> [!question] 1
> $x^2 \equiv\ 4\ mod\ 7$ 

| x             | 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   | 6   |
| ------------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| $x^2\ mod\ 7$ | 1   | 4   | 2   | 2   | 4   | 1   |

> [!note] ans of 1 
> $x\equiv 5\ mod\ 7\equiv 2\ mod \ 7$

> [!question] 2

| x              | 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   | 6   | 7   | 8   |
| -------------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| $x^2\ mod\ 17$ | 1   | 4   | 6   | 16  | 8   | 2   | 15  | 13  |
| x              | 9   | 10  | 11  | 12  | 13  | 14  | 15  | 16  |
| $x^2\ mod\ 17$ | 13  | 15  | 2   | 8   | 16  | 9   | 4   | 1   |
